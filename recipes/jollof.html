Jollof Rice

Home

A beloved West African dish with tomato-rich flavor.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Blend tomatoes, onions, and peppers.
  2. Cook the mixture with oil and spices.
  3. Add rice and simmer until done.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 5 tomatoes
  • Onions, garlic, oil, spices
